USM Haller Table

Modern Tables and Desks

USM Haller modern tables are available in a wide range of sizes, colors, and finishes, providing multi-use flexibility needed in today's home and office environments. An array of accessories such as wire management systems and modesty screens can be incorporated with USM Haller tables to provide a clean, modern aesthetic.

Design your own furniture with the USM configurator
Power strip

Power strip

Can be configured individually for data and power supply according to country-specific requirements. Available with a 4 m cable or a switch. Can be magnetically attached or integrated into the cable duct.

CPU holder

CPU holder

Height-adjustable, width is adjusted using Velcro. This provides space for computers of various sizes.

Pivoting pen holder

Pivoting pen holder

Provides room for writing instruments and can be discreetly stowed under the tabletop.

Cable routing

Cable routing

Can be clamped to the table leg and guides cables to the outlet.

Cable duct

Cable duct

Black aluminum profile to accommodate power strips (8) and cables. Cover can be opened from both sides. Table depths can be expanded up to the EU standard of more than 31 in. For tables with lengths of 79, 69, 60, 50, 40 and 30 in.

USM Privacy Panels

USM Privacy Panels

Can be attached to the back and sides to ensure individuality and privacy for people working at tables configured as a block station.

Front and side panels

Front and side panels

Can be attached to the back or the sides under the table. For tables with lengths of 79, 69, 60, 40 and 30 in.

Cable wire basket

Cable wire basket

Mounts invisibly under the tabletop.

Universal support

Universal support

Mounted to the table edge and serves as a holder for various accessories.

Flat screen pivot arm

Flat screen pivot arm

Screens are bolted onto a height-adjustable pivot arm via a fastening plate (VESA norm). This can be mounted onto the table edges with the universal support (1) or into the table leg using an assembly tube.

Link chain

Link chain

Takes up cables and guides them from the table to the outlet.

USM Haller Table

Integrated technology

USM Haller tables are ideally suited for use as modern desks, with accessories designed to intelligently marry technology and modern aesthetics. From pivot arms to privacy panels, wire management components to CPU holders, these modern desks maximize organization, privacy, and creativity while maintaining a sleek, modern aesthetic.

Why USM Haller Table?

Sleek design

Designed over 45 years ago, the USM Haller Table is furniture that embodies timeless elegance. Its clean lines allow it to harmonize perfectly with the modular furniture of the same name.

Surfaces

The consistent system concept offers room for something new, even for a design classic. Choose from a standard selection of 55 different surface materials. We also accept special requests.

Accessories

Useful accessories that are attached to the table expand its functionality and provide space for a variety of uses. An optional hinged cover on the table offers a clean solution for easy network access.

Ergonomics

Using a patented height adjustment feature, the tables can be freely adjusted from 27" to 34", thus adjusting to the individual needs of its users. This makes it ideally suited to desk sharing.
